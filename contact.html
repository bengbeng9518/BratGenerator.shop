Frequently Asked Questions

Before contacting us, you might find the answer to your question below:

How do I download my text design?

After creating your text effect, click the "Download Image" button. You can choose from PNG, JPEG, or SVG formats. The download will start automatically.

Is Brat Generator really free?

Yes! Brat Generator is completely free to use. There are no hidden costs, subscriptions, or watermarks on your downloads.

Can I use the generated images commercially?

Yes, you can use the images you create for both personal and commercial purposes. You retain full ownership of your creations.

Why isn't my design downloading?

Make sure your browser allows downloads and that you have sufficient storage space. Try refreshing the page and creating your design again. If the problem persists, contact our support team.

Can I suggest new features?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our users. Send us your feature suggestions using the contact form above or email us at hello@bratgenerator.shop.
